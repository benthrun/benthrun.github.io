Project for Aircraft Design

The aircraft design course I took had a project which required obtaining a solution to the AIAA design competition. The team I was on was tasked with several different performance metrics to meet, such as takeoff and landing distances, minimum cpacity, etc.

I specifically worked on the Propulsion as well as Aerodyanmic Performance. XFLR5 and MATLAB were softwares that I worked with to obtain useful information and manipulate data. Some of the tasks done I performed required making various trade studies as well as developing the flight envelope.

The team was succesful in producing an effective design, and was able to meet all of the performance metrics laid out in the Request for Proposal (RFP).
